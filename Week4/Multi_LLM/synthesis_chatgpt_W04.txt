# Week 4 Market Prediction Brief

## 1. WEEKLY REGIME

**Risk-on recovery with elevated volatility.**

The dominant regime is still bullish, but not cleanly aggressive. Technicals and macro support risk assets, while almanac factors warn of volatility.

## 2. INDEX OUTLOOK

| Index   |            Direction |    Estimated Range | Key Reason                                                      |
| ------- | -------------------: | -----------------: | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **SPX** |     Slightly bullish | **+0.3% to +1.2%** | Bullish structure remains intact above key moving averages.     |
| **NDX** |              Bullish | **+0.8% to +2.0%** | Strong momentum and Technology leadership support continuation. |
| **IWM** | Bullish but volatile | **+0.5% to +2.5%** | Small-cap outperformance suggests improving breadth.            |

## 3. SECTOR LEADERSHIP

**Leading sector:** Technology
**Lagging sector:** Utilities

Technology leadership matters because XLK outperformed strongly at **+2.50%**, supporting the Nasdaq-led risk-on thesis. Utilities lagging at **+0.40%** suggests the market is not rotating defensively yet.

## 4. CORE SYNTHESIS MATRIX

| Evidence Leg  | Signal           | Alignment                                   |
| ------------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Technical** | Bullish          | Strongly supports thesis                    |
| **Macro**     | Slightly bullish | Supports thesis, but with event risk        |
| **Almanac**   | Volatile risk-on | Partially supports, but warns of turbulence |

**Convergence:** Technical strength, falling yields, weaker USD, declining oil, and stronger IWM breadth all support risk-on continuation.

**Conflict:** Almanac seasonality, FOMC, options expiry, and compressed trading week create volatility risk.

## 5. TOP SUPPORTING REASON

The strongest evidence is **technical breadth improvement**, especially IWM outperforming at **+4.00%**, while SPX and NDX remain structurally bullish.

## 6. TOP CONTRADICTION / RISK

The biggest threat is **event-driven volatility** from Retail Sales, FOMC, BoE, BoJ, and options expiry.

## 7. INVALIDATION CONDITION

The thesis is invalidated if major support zones fail, especially if SPX loses its bullish EMA structure or NDX breaks below the stated **29,200 trendline support**.

## 8. TONE / CAVEAT LANGUAGE

Positioning should be **constructively bullish but not aggressive**. The evidence supports upside continuation, but the week should be treated as a headline-sensitive volatility window.
